About me

I'm now a graduate researcher in the AIMP-Lab at Northwestern University, advised by Prof.Bo Zhou, working on generative models for medical imaging translation and medical VLMs.

I earned my B.Eng. in Electronic and Electrical Engineering from the University of Birmingham, where I worked with Prof.Le Zhang.
