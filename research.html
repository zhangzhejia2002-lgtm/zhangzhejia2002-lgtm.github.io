Research focus

A few directions my work has centered on.

01

CT-Free PET Attenuation Correction

Diffusion-based generative methods for CT-free PET attenuation correction, with a focus on preserving absolute SUV quantification through explicit, physically-grounded feature guidance.

02

Missing-Modality Medical Imaging

Robust multi-modal frameworks that compensate for missing MRI modalities, enabling reliable diagnosis such as liver fibrosis staging from non-contrast T1WI, T2WI, and DWI.

03

Generative Image-to-Image Translation

A taxonomy of diffusion-based image-to-image translation — trajectory parameterization, condition injection, and the efficiency–fidelity trade-off — read through the lens of quantitative medical imaging.
